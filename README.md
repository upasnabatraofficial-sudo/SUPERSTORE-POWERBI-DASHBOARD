# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard — Power BI

An interactive sales dashboard built on the Sample Superstore retail dataset, designed to surface key business insights across regions, categories, and product performance.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop** — dashboard development and visualization
- **Dataset** — Sample Superstore (9,994 orders, 2014–2017)

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- **Total Sales:** $2.30M across 4 years
- **Total Profit:** $286.40K
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 38K units
- **West region** drives the highest sales, followed closely by East
- **Technology** is the top-performing category by sales
- **Canon imageCLASS** is the highest profit-generating product

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visuals

| Visual | Description |
|---|---|
| KPI Cards | Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity at a glance |
| Sales by Region | Bar chart comparing performance across West, East, Central, South |
| Sales Trend Over Time | Line chart showing monthly sales movement |
| Sales by Category | Treemap breaking down Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology |
| Top 10 Products by Profit | Horizontal bar chart of highest profit-generating products |

## 💡 Skills Demonstrated
- Data import and cleaning in Power Query

- Dashboard layout and stakeholder-ready formatting
- Trend identification and regional performance analysis
